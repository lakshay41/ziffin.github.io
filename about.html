What is the Ziffien story ?

What made Lakshay and Gagan build a platform that is Changing the way India eats?


It all started back in 2019 when two chitkarian graduates, Gagan Tuli and Lakshay Jawa decided they wanted to make life easier by changing the way India eats - all with just a tap! With their idea of ’hyperlocal food delivery’, all they needed was the tech to power it and were introduced to Gagan Tuli, who brought this vision to life with the first website. And with this, Ziffien was launched as a tiffin ordering & delivery platform.... In September of 2019, Ziffien began operations by signing up a few restaurants in chandiagrh . Soon enough, the first team of Hunger Saviors came into action to deliver food within 40 minutes. Fun fact: The technology that delivers great food right to your doorstep was completely developed in-house! With love and support from consumers, Ziffien expanded far and wide, first through the entire city of Chandigarh and then across the entire country. As they say, the rest is history. Today, Ziffien is the leading food ordering and delivery platform in India. The innovative technology, large and nimble delivery service, and exceptional consumer focus at Ziffen enabled a host of benefits that includes lightning fast deliveries, live order tracking and no restrictions on order amount, all while having the pleasure of enjoying your favourite meal wherever you'd like it.
